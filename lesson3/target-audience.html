Target Audience

This site is targeted to businesses in the whereabouts of the city of Sao Paulo and their owners.

Personas

Persona #1: a business owner well established in the local market, trying to make himself/herself available to making more business deals or arrengements around the city. This person is going to use this website via desktop or mobile.

Persona #2: someone that visits the website looking for local services and/or suppliers. This person has his/her own business or either work for one that is looking to make business deals, purchases arrengements. This person is going to use this website via desktop or mobile.

Scenarios

    These are only some of the possible scenarios that would make someone visit this site for.

  • As a business owner, I want to make my business available in the digital world.
  • As a business owner, I want to make business deals with others available around.
  • As someone who just happens to be living around, I would like to find a product or service nearby.
  • As a business owner, I would like to sign up to receive the site news.
  • As a visitor in the site, I would like to see the weather.
