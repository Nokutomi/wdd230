White Space and Clean Design

Hyer

flyhyer.com

The first impression you have here is that the plane is really in the clean sky. That happens because of the clean and minimalist design used in this page. There is not a lot of things and information going on, but the most important. This is a good example of how minimalist, clean design, and a good use of white spaces gives us a feel of lightness and freedom.

CSS-Tricks

css-tricks.com

CSS-Tricks mobile homepage uses contrast beautifully. You can see the background colors close to black / dark-gray, and the important navigation links and Logo in white, making a great contrast.

Visual Hierarchy

Google

google.com

The famous Google search page is very simple and straight forward in its usability thanks to visual hierarchy. In the main part of the page, you get a huge "Google" to let you know you're on google. Just after that, a search box which takes the whole width of the page. Later on we have a "Trending searchers" section, and we understand this is a section because the options just below come a little bit smaller, which binds them in the hierarchy just below the title.
